### Recommended Learning Path
1. Start with **GitHub Copilot Foundation** to build a strong understanding of the basics
2. Progress to **GitHub Copilot Advanced Features** once comfortable with fundamental concepts
3. Focus on areas most relevant to your role (individual developer, team lead, or enterprise administrator)
4. Practice hands-on with the tutorials and examples provided in the documentation
